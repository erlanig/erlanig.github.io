About Me

Hi everybody! Allow me to introduce myself, my name is Erlan Irhab Ghalib. I am a 5th semester S1 Informatics student at Diponegoro University. My Experiences

Forum Silaturahmi Mahasiswa Kuningan-Semarang (Forsmaka) is a student organization in the Kuningan Regency area in Semarang City. Forsmaka adheres to the principle of kinship which hopes to unite Kuningan overseas students in Semarang.

HMIF (Informatics Student Associations) is a student association organization of the Department of Informatics, Faculty of Science of Mathematics, Diponegoro University. HMIF Undip acts as a forum that facilitates the activities of all students of Informatics.

Participated in the Virtual Big Data Analytics Internship Experience at Kimia Farma and successfully completed all tasks related to Big Data Analytics from Kimia Farma, such as Data warehousing, Data analytics, SQL Querying, and Big Data Processing and Scheduling.

Forsmaka Sharing and Chare (Forshare) is a charity event for orphans which is held annually at an orphanage in the Kuningan, West Java. Get a total donation of 200% of the target.

Forsmaka Gathering (Forsathering) or First Gathering Is an annual event held after the acceptance of new students with the aim of introducing Forsmaka to new students and aiming to get to know each other. Design a Forsgathering 2022 banner.

Informatics Concept Socialization (I-Console) is assistance for new Undip Informatics students, PMB Faculties and Departments, sharing, trainings oriented to new students 2022, LKMMPD, and Guilds. Received the "Best Pemanduan" award.
